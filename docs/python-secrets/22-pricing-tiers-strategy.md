## Pricing Philosophy

### 1. Free Tier is Genuinely Free
- **No time limits** - Free courses don't expire
- **No credit card required** - Start learning immediately
- **No hidden upsells** - Free content is complete, not crippled
- **No "freemium" tricks** - What's free stays free

**Why?**
- Builds trust and goodwill
- Allows users to validate teaching quality
- Creates word-of-mouth marketing
- Demonstrates value before asking for payment

### 2. Clear Value at Each Tier
Each paid tier must offer **obvious, tangible value**:

**Standard ($149)**:
- Cost: ~$2.50/hour of content
- Value: Learn skills that increase salary by $10K-20K/year
- ROI: 10x-100x return on investment

**Premium ($299)**:
- Cost: ~$2.50/hour of content + community + support
- Value: Senior-level skills + job opportunities + network
- ROI: Career transformation in 3-6 months

### 3. Premium Tier = Premium Experience
Premium isn't just "more content" - it's a **better experience**:
- Community access (high value for networking)
- Personalized support (code reviews, Q&A)
- Exclusive content (not available elsewhere)
- Updates forever (continuous value)

## Money-Back Guarantee

### Policy
- **30-day money-back guarantee** on all paid tiers
- No questions asked for first 7 days
- After 7 days: valid reason required (didn't meet expectations)
- Refund processed within 5 business days

### Why Offer This?
- Reduces purchase risk
- Builds trust
- Increases conversion rate
- Low actual refund rate expected (if content is quality)

### Abuse Prevention
- Limit to 1 refund per user
- Track repeat refunders
- Require feedback for refund

## Competitor Response Monitoring

### If Competitors Lower Prices
- Don't engage in price wars

### If Competitors Raise Prices
- Consider gradual increase (stay slightly cheaper)

- Double down on unique SE content
